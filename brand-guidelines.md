# TechMiner Global — Brand Guidelines (Starter)

Brand assets and quick usage rules.

## Logo
- Primary logo file: `public/logo.png` (replace with original high-res PNG or SVG where possible).
- Always keep a minimum clear space equal to the height of the "TM" mark around the logo.

## Colors
- Primary green: `#00FF66` (use this for CTAs and accents)
- Dark background: `#040404`
- Neutral text: `#E6FFF2`

Use gradients from `#00FF66` → `#00CC99` for hero accents.

## Typography
- Headings: `Inter` or `Montserrat` (bold)
- Body: `Inter` (regular)

## Assets included
- `public/logo.png` — main logo
- `public/favicon-32x32.png` — favicon placeholder
- `public/social-banner-1200x630.svg` — Open Graph / Twitter card
- `public/social-banner-1500x500.svg` — wider banner
- `assets/business-card.svg` — business card mockup (SVG)
- `public/email-signature.html` — HTML signature template

## File formats & export
- Replace `logo.png` with an SVG for best scaling if you have it.
- Export favicons at 16x16, 32x32 and `favicon.ico` that contains multiple sizes.
- Export social banners as PNG for social networks if needed (use the SVG templates in a browser and `Export`).

## Usage do's and don'ts
- Do keep high contrast between logo and background.
- Don't distort or recolor the logo outside the approved palette.
